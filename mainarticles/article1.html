The Undead Plague: Capturing the Horror of a Zombie Virus in Photos

Photos taken under the microscope unveil the microscopic world of viruses, shedding light on their danger and intriguing beauty. These images reveal the complex structures and mechanisms of viruses, offering insights into the scientific study of infectious diseases. They remind us of the ongoing battle between humans and these tiny, formidable foes, while also highlighting the aesthetic allure of the unseen.
